## Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for Students

### ✅ Do’s:
- Think before posting any type of content online.
- Use privacy settings to protect your personal information.
- Be respectful and professional in your interactions with anyone.
- Always verify information before sharing it.
- Maintain a positive digital footprint.

### ❌ Don’ts:
- Do not share sensitive personal information such as address, phone number, etc.
- Avoid posting offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content.
- Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments.
- Avoid spreading fake news or unverified information.
- Do not overshare details about your personal life with anyone.
