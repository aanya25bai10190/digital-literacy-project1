# Prevention Checklist for Students

### ✅ Do’s:
1. Always verify the sender before responding to any kind of payment or UPI request.  
2. Keep your UPI PIN and banking credentials private; never share them with anyone.  
3. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) for all digital accounts.  
4. Use strong, unique passwords for email, social media, and banking apps.  
5. Keep your phone and apps updated with the latest security patches.  
6. Only download apps from official app stores.  
7. Think before sharing any kind of personal information online. For example-phone number, address, UPI ID, etc.  
8. Use privacy settings on social media to control who can see your posts.  

### ❌ Don’ts:
1. Do not click on unknown links in messages or emails.  
2. Do not respond to suspicious payment requests or OTP messages.  
3. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or financial transactions.  
4. Do not overshare personal details on any social media platform.  
5. Never trust calls or messages claiming urgent bank issues without verification.  
6. Do not reuse passwords across multiple accounts.  
7. Avoid downloading pirated software or apps from untrusted websites.  
8. Do not ignore security alerts or app permissions.

### 🛡 Reporting Channels:
- **National Cyber Crime Portal:** [cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in)  
- **Helpline:** 1930
